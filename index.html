Choose a Hub section, find a credible link, and submit it with a short justification.

What counts as a good link

  • Credible source (universities, official docs, reputable organizations or platforms)
  • Clear and student-friendly
  • Stable and dependable (avoid personal blogs or social threads)
  • Fits a specific Hub section

Links to avoid

  • Personal blogs or Medium posts
  • Hype or shortcut content
  • Anything that replaces student thinking
  • Unstable or spammy sources

How it works

  1. Browse the Hub sections to understand what each needs.
  2. Pick a section.
  3. Find a link that fits the guidance.
  4. Submit it with a short explanation in the workspace.
